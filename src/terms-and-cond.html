Terms and conditions

I provide clinical hypnotherapy, NLP and other talking therapies. My primary responsibility is to protect the health and well-being of you as my client, and to guide and support you through the safe practice of these therapies.

Conditions

I provide therapy on the basis of the following conditions:

  • None of the suggestions or techniques I may use will cause you harm.
  • The therapy I provide will add to whatever other support/medication you may already have in place for your well-being.
  • While I will use my professional skills and training to provide therapy to the best of my ability, the results will vary between individuals and cannot be guaranteed.
  • I will respect your dignity, individuality and privacy, and whatever you disclose in a therapy session will remain confidential, except that:
    • In certain situations, I may be legally obliged to share information (such as if I were concerned that you present a danger to yourself or others and need outside assistance); and
    • As part of my professional support arrangements, I may share the nature of your situation and the therapy proposed or provided with my supervisor or with other professional advisors (omitting all personal details that could identify you).
  • I respect your right to be involved in decisions about your therapy and care and will communicate with you throughout the course of the therapy about options and progress.
  • You are fully accountable for your actions towards yourself or towards others, and I will not be held accountable for anything that you may choose to do after a session.

Payment by bank transfer or cash is expected at the time of the session.
