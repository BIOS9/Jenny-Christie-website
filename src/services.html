What's involved in a consultation

Where:

Wellington.

Times I'm available:

Friday, Saturday, Sunday 11am - 7pm.

Step 1 - initial contact:

Get in touch by phone/text or email, and we can sort out a time for an introductory consultation.

Step 2 - introductory consultation:

This is essentially a conversation where we talk about the issue you want to work on. We'll talk about things such as how you experience it, how it impacts on your life, any particular things or circumstances that make it more or less of a problem for you. This helps us define what needs to change. We can talk about what I can help with and my initial thoughts on how I might go about that.
It's good to meet up and do it face-to-face, but if necessary, this can be done over the phone.
Ater the initial consultation, you can decide whether you want to go any further.
The introductry consultation is free.

Step 3 - therapy sessions:

The therapy sessions are where the therapy actually happens. We will talk about what I have planned to do and we'll make sure that you understand what is invovled and are comfortable with it. Usually then I'll guide you into hypnosis, and use therapeutic suggestions to help you work through the issue. Obviously I'll bring you out of the hypnosis well before the end of the session.
A therapy session will take between 50 minutes and 90 minutes.
Depending on the issue(s) being worked on, you will typically need about three sessions scheduled about a week apart.
The standard cost of each therapy session is $120 (enquire about case-by-case variations if you are a student or unwaged etc.).

Step 4 - follow-up:

Hypnotherapy works on your patterns of thinking, and often it takes a while for the new patterns to settle in. I like to check in with you about how it's going after a few weeks (or sooner if you like).
Up to three follow-up phone calls are included in the cost of a set of therapy session(s).
